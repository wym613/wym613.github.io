Doodle Dynamic Recognition Based on CNN

Our main task is to receive a draw as an input and to predict in the highest success rate what is drawn in it from a list of labels that we will train our algorithm over it. The CNN was trained to recognize 11 classes using the quick draw dataset. It achieves top 5 error. The table will show the top 5 predictions.
